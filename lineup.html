Lineup

This year we have an excellent lineup that will be performing and exhilerating the crowds with vibrant and fresh energy.
Events such as these lend themselves to sunshine and fun times, after all.

KRS-One

Edutainment: Hip Hop as intelligent movement

KRS will begin with a performance of timeless classics from the days of Boogie Down Productions and Afrika Bambaata, followed by a lecture on the tenets of Hip Hop and the importance of women in the culture.

About KRS-One

A Hip Hop pioneer and legend, KRS-One was there since the beginning. Known as 'The Teacher', KRS has been a spokesman and torch holder for Hip Hop culture in the US and the world.

The highly influential 'Stop the Violence' movement sent ripples across the world as campaigns to stop violence on the streets and in the mind.

Phife Dawg

The Original Funky Diabetic

The late Phife Dawg will be blessing the stage, bringing fresh rhymes and sage advice to the youth

About Phife Dawg

Phife, the Five Foot Assassin, rose to fame in the early '90s with the jazz-infused East Coast hip hop vibes of A Tribe Called Quest.

With a penchant for some raggamuffin music and funky flow, Phife is considered a hip hop legend.
